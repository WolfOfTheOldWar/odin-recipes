Bronco Burger

These burgers are pretty easy to make. They can certainly be filling if made into thick patties!

  1. 5 fresh jalapeno peppers
  2. 4 pounds ground beef
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. 1 egg
  5. ¼ cup steak sauce, (e.g. Heinz 57)
  6. ¼ cup minced white onion
  7. 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (e.g. Tabasco™)
  8. 1 pinch dried oregano
  9. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  10. 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  11. ¼ cup crushed Fritos® corn chips
  12. 8 large potato hamburger buns
  13. 8 slices pepperjack cheese
  1. Step 1 Preheat a grill for high heat. When the grill is hot, roast the jalapeno peppers until blackened on all sides. Place in a plastic bag to sweat and loosen the blackened skin. Rub the skin off, then seed if desired, and chop.
  2. Step 2 In a large bowl, use your hands to mix together the chopped jalapenos, ground beef, salt, pepper, egg, steak sauce, onion, hot pepper sauce, oregano, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt and Fritos®. Divide into 8 balls, and flatten into patties.
  3. Step 3 Grill patties for 10 to 15 minutes, turning once, or until well done. I always drink one beer, then flip, drink another beer, then remove from the grill and place on buns. Top each one with a slice of pepperjack cheese and pig out!
